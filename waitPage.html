How LGHS Chess Works

LGHS Chess is played between two schools. Anyone at the two participating schools can play. You can submit a move at any point during your school’s turn in the game.

Once time is up, votes are tallied up and the move with the most votes is made on the board. Note, at the end of school 2’s move, the votes will be tallied, but not displayed until the start of LGHS’s voting period the following morning to ensure each school has the same amount of decision-making time.

How To Play LGHS Chess

To play, you must sign-in to your school-provided Google account. At the bottom of the screen, the display will show which school is to move. If it is your school’s move, click and drag your school’s chess piece to a valid square. You may undo your move anytime PRIOR TO pressing the “Submit” button. To undo your move, press the “UNDO” button directly below the chess board. When you are ready to finalize your move, press the “SUBMIT” button below the Google sign-in. You may only submit one move per turn cycle.
